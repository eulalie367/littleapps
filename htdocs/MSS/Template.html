Welcome to Affordable Roofing Online
Servicing Santa Clara County, San Benito, Santa Cruz, Monterey County, California areas!

We are a Certified Bay Area Green Business located in Morgan Hill, California, and are referred to as one of the top 3% best steep slope Contractors in the Country. Affordable Roofing has been featured in Western Roofing Magazine and Professional Roofing Contractor, winning National Awards by GAF/ELK for Installation Excellence and Consumer Protection. 

We serve Santa Clara, San Benito, Santa Cruz & Monterey counties. Affordable Roofing is family owned and operated. Richard and Pamela Garcia, owners of Affordable Roofing, have earned their Reputation as #1 in South County by providing high-quality roofing services to our community for the past 14 years.

Richard Garcia, Contractor has been in the roofing industry for over 35 years. Richard's experience contributes to the successful completion of each roofing project. Pamela Garcia brings over 25 years of business management, and a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business Administration. She oversees the business operations and runs the company with passion and a commitment to excellence. Their son, Jonathan Garcia is a graduate of San Jose State University with a Bachelor's Degree in Environmental Studies and Alternative Energy.  Jonathan manages the solar department as well as his own company, Signature Solar and Design.

Our roofers are drug tested for your safety and factory certified  upholding highest roofing standards.  Our work is backed by some of the largest roofing manufacturers in the Country, allowing us to offer customers the best warranties available.

We are a Certified "Master Elite" Contractcr by GAF/ELK, the largest shingle Manufacturer in the Country also, Certified by Eagle Tile to install "Solar Save integrated tile roofs".  We are green friendly and assist our clients by installing tubular skylights, Low-E radiant barrier insulation, solar attic fans and many other cool roof systems (See tabs above)

We are fully insured and bonded. Workers Compensation and General Liability.
