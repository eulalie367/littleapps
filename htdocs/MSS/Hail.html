Don't gamble with the equity in your home!

The equity in your home is not cosmetic! If you sell your home later the hail damage will be notes in the inspection and the buyer will want you to pay for a new roof out your pocket.

Q. How do you know if you have hail damage?

A. if marble size or large hail hit your home then chances are there is assorted damage to screms, soft metal, roof vents, gutters, and roof shingles.

Q. That's just cosmetic damage. yor roof look fine from the groound...., why Modern Storm solution contractor recomended filing an insurance claim?

A. because your homeowners insurance policy encourages you to file a claim any time that you have sudden accidental damages to your property.

Q. It seems a waste of time. l can't see any roof damage and a roofer said the damages was minimal. does Modern Storm Solution contractor think l should still file a claim?

A. Yes we do! The roofer is not properly trained to inspect the minimal cosmetic type damages that hail can cause ...., only your insurance adjuster and other claim professionals can determine the exent of the damage.
